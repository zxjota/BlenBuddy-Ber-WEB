1) Introduction

Welcome to BlenBuddy Ber (the “App”). We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App in the United States. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use the App.

2) Scope & Audience

This policy applies to personal information collected by BlenBuddy Ber in connection with your use of the App and its services. It does not apply to services provided by third parties not controlled by us.

3) Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account & Profile: Name, email, and preferences if you create an account.
  • Recipes & Ingredients: Personal recipes, ingredients, notes, and tags you enter.
  • Support & Communication: Messages or attachments you provide when contacting us.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & App Data: Device type, OS version, app version, language, settings, and performance diagnostics.
  • Usage Data: App features used, screens viewed, and interactions to improve user experience.

C. Information from Third Parties

  • App Store Providers: Subscription or purchase history (we do not receive full payment details).
  • Analytics/Crash Services: Aggregated or pseudonymous data to improve app quality.

D. Sensitive Information

Please avoid entering sensitive personal information (e.g., SSN, health records). We do not intentionally collect such information.

4) How We Use Information

  • Provide, personalize, and maintain the App and your recipes.
  • Process purchases and manage subscriptions.
  • Analyze usage to optimize performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Communicate updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

5) Legal Bases

Where applicable, we rely on: (i) contract performance (providing the App), (ii) legitimate interests (improving and securing the App), (iii) consent (permissions you grant), and/or (iv) legal obligations.

6) How We Share Information

We do not sell or share personal information for advertising purposes. We may disclose data to:

  • Service Providers: Vendors processing data on our behalf.
  • Payment Processors & App Stores: For purchases and subscription management.
  • Legal & Safety: To comply with law or protect rights, safety, and the App.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or asset sales, subject to this policy.

7) Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as needed to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion at any time (see Contact Us).

8) Security

We implement measures to protect personal data. No method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9) Children’s Privacy

The App is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact us to delete it.

10) U.S. State Privacy Rights

Depending on your state, you may have rights under laws such as CCPA/CPRA. These rights may include access, deletion, correction, and opting out of data sales/sharing. We do not sell personal information.

11) Do Not Track
